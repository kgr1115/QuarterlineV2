# Product Brief

Date: 2026-05-09

## Product

QuarterlineV2 is a planned downloadable desktop app for commercial real estate
research and reporting.

## Target User

Institutional CRE research analysts and research-led investment, brokerage, and
asset-management teams.

## Core Problem

Quarterly CRE reports require analysts to reconcile fragmented market data,
property details, financial assumptions, broker intelligence, source documents,
and local judgment. That work is often scattered across spreadsheets, PDFs,
emails, maps, notes, and presentation files.

## Product Thesis

The quarterly report should be the natural output of continuous research work
inside one durable desktop workspace.

## V2 Positioning

QuarterlineV2 should feel like an institutional research terminal for CRE
analysis: local-first, precise, dense, secure, and built around evidence-backed
report generation.

## Principles

- Desktop-first, not browser-first.
- Local-first, with hosted capabilities added only by decision.
- Evidence before narrative.
- Analyst judgment stays in control.
- Reports should be clean outside and traceable inside.
- Dense information should remain readable.
- AI should summarize and structure, not invent.
- Confidential file handling is a core product boundary.

## Current Non-Claim

No V2 product has been implemented yet.
